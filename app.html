BlossomBuds: A coding Challenge

Given two values, display the numbers 1-100. When a number is divisible by the first value, display "Blossom" instead. When a number is divisible by the second value, display "Buds" instead. When a number is divisible by both values, display "BlossomBuds".

Instructions

Enter a blossom value, buds value and stop value. Press the button, and see "Blossom", "Buds", and "BlossomBuds" replace the numbers accordingly.
